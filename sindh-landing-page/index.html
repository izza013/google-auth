About Sindh Ombudsman

The Sindh Ombudsman office serves as an independent authority to address citizen complaints against government departments and public officials. Our AI-assisted system makes it easier than ever to file and track your complaints.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can file a complaint?

Any citizen of Sindh can file a complaint against government departments or public officials.

How long does the process take?

Complaints are typically reviewed within 30 days of submission.

Is there a fee for filing?

No, filing a complaint with the Sindh Ombudsman is completely free.
